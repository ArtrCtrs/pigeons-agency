Launch Expeditions with Seeds to collect pigeons in your Aviary.
Pigeons generate Droppings. Sell pigeons to collect Feathers.
Use ressources (seeds, droppings and feathers) to Upgrade your seeds generation, number of max pigeons, and ressources storage.

Don't forget to set your pigeons as attackers and/or defenders to Attack other players and defend yourself from attacks.

You have a maximum of 5 attackers and 5 defenders. Attacks have a 1 minute cooldown. An attacked player can't be attacked for 15 minutes.
